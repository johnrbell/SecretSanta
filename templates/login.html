Hi, welcome to EAF Cafe-Regs Secret Santa.

How It Works:

You sign up. When we close registration we will assign Secret Santas. Once Secret Santas are assigned, we will reach out to all users and ask them to upload their addresses.

When your address is uploaded, it is encrypted using your Secret Santa's public key. This way only your Secret Santa can decrypt it.

Don't lose your password you create for your key: if you do, you won't be able to decrypt the address sent to you (we store your encrypted key on our servers).

If you have any problems please DM ross3777 on discord.


All data will be deleted after the Secret Santa ends.
